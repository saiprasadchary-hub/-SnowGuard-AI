EMERGENCY MODE

You've activated SnowGuard AI Emergency Mode. Follow these survival instructions:

1. Stay Calm & Assess Situation

Check for injuries and take stock of your resources. Don't panic - help is available.

2. Find or Create Shelter

Protect yourself from wind and cold. Use natural windbreaks or build a snow cave if necessary.

3. Prevent Hypothermia

Keep moving fingers and toes. Layer clothing. Stay dry at all costs.

4. Signal for Help

Use bright colors, mirrors, or create signals in the snow. Three of anything is a universal distress signal.

Winter Safety Tips

  • Dress in layers to trap body heat
  • Keep emergency supplies in your vehicle
  • Check weather forecasts regularly
  • Avoid traveling during winter storms
  • Learn to recognize signs of frostbite and hypothermia
